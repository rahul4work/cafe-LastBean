Refund Policy - Cafe LastBean

Eligibility for Refunds

We strive to provide the best experience. However, if you're not satisfied, you may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • The issue is reported within 24 hours of the order time.
  • The item delivered is incorrect, spoiled, or not as described.
  • You provide proof (photos or order details) of the issue.

Non-Refundable Items

Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for:

  • Change of mind after the item is prepared or dispatched.
  • Incorrect orders due to customer error at checkout.
  • Delays caused by third-party delivery services once dispatched.

Refund Process

If approved, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days via the original payment method. We'll notify you via email once the refund has been initiated.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or concerns, don’t hesitate to reach out to our team:

Email: refunds@cafelastbean.com
